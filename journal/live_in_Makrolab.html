Live in Makrolab

This is a live recording of an improvised performance done in the Makrolab during a residency of the dyne.org crews. It is 2 hours long and includes electronic music tracks by Dixan (spinningkids), IT-Alien and Delazey, remixed with live percussion and voice.

Musicians involved:

  • Stefano "Shezzan" Cinti at the congas
  • Davide Pinna playing with circuit-bending and voice
  • Me mixing electronic music and voice

Only a low quality live recording is available (recorded and streamed using MuSE), unfortunately it is encoded in the proprietary MP3 format (Ogg/Vorbis was not yet ready at that time): live_in_makrolab.mp3
