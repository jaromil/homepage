Denis "Jaromil" Roio - Inventor, Ph.D.

Hi, I'm Jaromil

Developing free and open source software since 1999, made headlines with ASCII art and projects like dyne:bolic, Devuan GNU+Linux and the forkbomb artwork.

Leading Dyne.org think &do tank within Europe flagship digital research projects and envisioning the horizon of future research grants.

Intrigued by tech innovation, minimalism and environmental sustainability, cryptography and the elegance of algorithms.

Seeking to be inspired, to facilitate the shift to a commons-based society, to work hard for purposes that are worth it and to be surrounded by team players.

Say hi on twitter @jaromil - write me a mail J@Dyne.org.
